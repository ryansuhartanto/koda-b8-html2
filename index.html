FumoFumo

FumoFumo (ふもふも), also known as Fumo or Fumos, are a line of plush toys manufactured and released by the company Gift. Originally designed by Nejikirio, it was named after the Japanese onomatopoeia "mofu mofu" (もふもふ) which is used to describe something fluffy or soft.

History

The FumoFumo plush was based off of Nejikirio's artwork and style. The iconic eye shape goes back to her earliest works, however the first time the more chibi, plush-like design was used, was in the doujinshi 'Together with Inu Sakuya' (いぬさくやといっしょ) released by the circle BLACK†ANGEL (currently known as COSMIC★COMICS) on the 31st of December 2006 at Comic Market 71. Though at the time, the term FumoFumo had not yet been used.

In August of 2008, Gift released the first two FumoFumo plushies of Reimu and Marisa. Following the initial release selling out, Gift went on to re-release Reimu and Marisa many times while also releasing new Fumos over the coming years continuing to this day.

In 2009, Nejikirio's circle (at the time known as ANGELTYPE), produced and sold a FumoFumo plush of Inu Sakuya. While not listed on Gift's website, it was made in partnership with them.

Also in 2009, Gift began to produce versions of their FumoFumo line that were four times larger than the standard sized versions referred to as Deka (でか) or DekaFumo (でかふも). Due to their size and increased cost, they would be produced in more limited quantities. Over the course of 2009-2012, DekaFumo versions of Reimu, Marisa, Cirno, Remilia and Flandre were sold many times. Though after 2012, no DekaFumo would be sold for 8 years. This extended time in combination with an increase in the popularity of Fumos overseas led to the original DekaFumo becoming extremely costly to anyone looking to purchase one secondhand. In 2020 new DekaFumo would begin to be produced.

The 17th Fumo to be produced by Gift was of Kaguya, sold in August of 2011. This was the first they made using a newer manufacturing/design process, resulting in the first 16 being known as "version 1"s, or "v1"s in the English speaking community. Gift would later release updated versions of the original 16, referring to them as ver.2 for Sanae, and ver.1.5 for the remaining 15. The final "v1.5" to be released was Sakuya. First being sold in November 2023, 11 years after the final time her v1 was sold officially.

Sanae was not the only character to receive a ver.2 Fumo, as Marisa also had one produced. However, rather than being an updated version of her original design, it featured a completely new look based on her appearance in Undefined Fantastic Object. Because of this, many fans refer to her as either “v2 Marisa” or “UFO Marisa.”
